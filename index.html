About Me

I am a software engineer, interested in Machine Learning and AI. Currently I work as an ML Engineer at Vernacular.ai, building applications using ML for Conversational AI and Spoken Language Understanding.

I have an affinity towards the research aspects of Machine Learning. My research experience so far has come from my time working as a research assistant in Image and Video Processing Lab (Prof. Suvendu Rup, IIIT Bhubaneswar) and Robotics Research Center (Prof. K Madhava Krishna, IIIT Hyderabad).

I am always happy to connect with people interested in disussing any of these topics or anything around Machine Learning. You can find me on LinkedIn or via e-mail.
