About Me

Hello, I'm Himansu Didwania - a passionate developer, football fan, metalhead, and Cosmere nerd.

Currently, I am a part of the Machine Learning team at AI4Bharat, where I am working on some exciting research problems in the domains of speech and NLP. Before this, I was an ML Engineer at Skit.ai. I also started a venture called Dreamscale AI, aiming to tackle some ambitious challenges with AI, but unfortunately, it didn't take off.

I love working on challenging ML problems, and my natural inclination is to approach them from a research perspective. During my undergrad studies, I was a research assistant in the Image and Video Processing Lab at IIIT Bhubaneswar and the Robotics Research Center at IIIT Hyderabad.

I am also interested in entrepreneurship and aspire to create a startup that solves real-world challenges. I am always on the lookout for interesting collaborations on ML projects and startup ideas.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or via e-mail. I also maintain my programming projects on GitHub, although I must admit that it's not always updated as frequently as I'd like. Let's chat about Machine Learning, Research, Startups, Football, or anything else that interests you.
