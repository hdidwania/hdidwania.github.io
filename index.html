About Me

I am a computer engineer interested in working in field of Deep Learning and AI. Currently I am an ML Engineer at Vernacular.ai working on the topics coversational AI and spoken language understanding.

My interest in Deep Learning comes from a research perspective, as I worked as research assistant in academic labs during my undergrad years. I was fortunate to be guided by Prof. Suvendu Rup in Image and Video Processing Lab, IIIT Bhubaneswar and Prof. K Madhava Krishna in Robotics Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad.

I am always looking to connect with practitioners, engineers and researchers working on Deep Learning. You can connect with me on LinkedIn or via e-mail.
