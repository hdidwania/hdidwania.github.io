About Me

I am Himansu Didwania, a software engineer interested in Machine Learning and AI. Currently I work as an ML Engineer at Skit, where I work on using Spoken Language Understanding for building voice bots. I completed B.Tech from IIIT Bhubaneswar in 2020.

I like to explore and work upon the research aspects of Machine Learning. During my undergrad, I have worked as research assistant in Image and Video Processing Lab, IIIT Bhubaneswar and Robotics Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad. Although I am still in the phase of exploring Machine Learning, my current interests broadly revolve around Generative Models and Representation Learning.

When not working on ML, I like to study maths and algorithms. I am working on some projects to fill my empty GitHub profile. Outside programming and computer science, I enjoy heavy metal music, for which I am trying to learn playing guitar (and terribly so). I like to read fiction- mostly scifi and fantasy genres. I am also an avid fan of Football and Professional Wrestling.

I can be reached via e-mail or LinkedIn. For my programming projects, you can visit my GitHub.
