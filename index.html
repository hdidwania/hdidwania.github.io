About Me

I am Himansu Didwania - a developer, football fan, metalhead, and Cosmere nerd.
Currently, I work in the Machine Learning team at AI4Bharat, working on some exciting research problems in the domains of speech and NLP. Prior to this, I worked as an ML Engineer at Skit.ai. Beyond these, I made an unsuccessful attempt to build Dreamscale AI.

I enjoy working on challenging ML problems and have been inclined towards approaching problems from research perspectives. During my undergrad, I worked as a research assistant in the Image and Video Processing Lab, at IIIT Bhubaneswar and Robotics Research Center, at IIIT Hyderabad.

I am also interested in entrepreneurship and want to build a startup of my own, and solve some challenging problems which can impact many.

I will be pleased to connect with you for a discussion on Machine Learning, Research, Startups, Football, or anything else. I am actively looking out for collaborations on ML projects and startup ideas and would love to engage with you on these. I can be reached via e-mail or LinkedIn. I maintain my programming projects on GitHub, though it is distressingly outdated.
